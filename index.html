About Me

I have worked on a variety of projects and will continue to do so throughout my time in the program.

I believe that my passion for web development, commitment to my continuing education in computer science, and strong skills in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript make me the ideal candidate for a position as Front End Developer.

I look forward to having the opportunity to speak with you further about how I can be an asset to your team.

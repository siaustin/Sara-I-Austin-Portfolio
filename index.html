About Me

Hello! My name is Sara Austin and I live in Raleigh, NC. I am a graduate of the University of North Carolina's Coding Boot Camp with a certification in full stack web development. I have a B.A. from The University of Alabama.

During my time in coding boot camp, I learned a variety of different technical skills. I learned to work in a collaborative environment with a focus on pair programming. I am certified in the following programming skills: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, jQuery, Javascript, Ajax, Firebase, and Node.js, MySQL, Express.js, Heroku, Handlebars.js, Sequelize, MongoDB, Mongoose, React.

I enjoy being challenged and engaging with projects that require me to work outside my comfort and knowledge set, as continuing to learn new languages and development techniques. I believe that my passion for web development, and strong skills in HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript make me the ideal candidate as a developer.

I look forward to having the opportunity to speak with you further about how I can be an asset to your team.
